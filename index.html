    How to Use

    1. Download Instagram Data:

    - Request your data from Instagram under Settings > Accounts Center > Your information and permissions > Download your information.

    - Important: Ensure that you download the data in JSON format rather than HTML format. The app only supports JSON files.


    2. Upload Files:

    Upload your followers_1.json and following.json files using the file inputs on this page.


    3. Check Results:

    Click the "Check who doesn't follow back" button to see a list of users you follow who do not follow you back. Each username will be displayed as a clickable link to their Instagram profile.
